# AI Platform Prediction

**My bet: Google DeepMind**

Not because of raw capability — Anthropic and OpenAI will remain neck-and-neck there — but because of distribution. Google owns the browser, the phone OS, the search engine, and the cloud. They've stumbled before (Bard, anyone?), but a company that can embed AI into 3 billion Android devices and every Chrome tab doesn't need to win the model race to win the platform war.

The real wildcard is that "most widely accepted" may not mean "most talked about in tech circles." It may mean the AI your grandmother uses without knowing it's AI. That's Google's game to lose.

*Written March 2026. Check back in five years.*
